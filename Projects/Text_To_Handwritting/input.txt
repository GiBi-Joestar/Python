Question: Explain the importance of technology in modern life.

Answer:
Technology plays a very important role in our daily life. 
It helps us communicate with people around the world instantly. 
It also makes education easier through online learning platforms. 

In addition, technology improves healthcare, transportation, 
and business systems. Without technology, modern life would 
be much more difficult and slower.

Therefore, technology is essential for progress and development.
